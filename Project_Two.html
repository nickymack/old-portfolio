Meet The Contestants!

Ronald the rabbit is a tier-two silver class running champion, holding the county record for most jumps in a 5k dash. In his spare time he enjoys tinkering in the rabbit den and watching World War Two documertaries.

Thomas the Turle, or "Tommy" (as his friends call him) is a gentle fellow. While strolling about town, Tommy loves to think of new puns and trying them out on his friends. Lately, he's really been "coming out of his shell."

Gale has been training for this race in secret for months. When she found out Farrah was a contestant, she knew she had to enter if only to beat that pompous little snot. When pressed for details on the falling out between the former friends, Gale's only response is: "she knows what she did."

Farrah Fox is running to raise awareness for the overuse of stereotypes against her species, including phrases like "crazy as a fox." When asked about her relationship with Gale, F. Fox only replied: "My main concern is to raise awareness, but I will tell you this: that goat is one top-knotch A-hole."
